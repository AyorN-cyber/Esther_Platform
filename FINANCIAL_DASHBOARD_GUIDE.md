## What is the Financial Dashboard?

The Financial Dashboard helps you track all income (revenue) and expenses related to your music ministry. It's a simple accounting tool to monitor your financial health.

## How It Works

### 1. **Adding Revenue (Income)**
Click the **"+ Revenue"** button to record money coming in:

**Examples of Revenue:**
- YouTube ad revenue
- Streaming royalties (Spotify, Apple Music)
- Concert/performance fees
- Merchandise sales
- Donations/offerings
- Sponsorships
- Music licensing fees

**What to Enter:**
- **Date:** When you received the money
- **Category:** Type of income (e.g., "YouTube Revenue", "Concert Fee", "Merchandise")
- **Amount:** How much money (in NGN - Nigerian Naira)
- **Description:** Optional notes
- **Recurring:** Check if this is regular income (e.g., monthly streaming)

### 2. **Adding Expenses**
Click the **"+ Expense"** button to record money going out:

**Examples of Expenses:**
- Studio rental fees
- Equipment purchases (microphones, cameras)
- Video production costs
- Marketing/advertising
- Website hosting
- Travel expenses for performances
- Costume/wardrobe
- Collaboration fees

**What to Enter:**
- **Date:** When you spent the money
- **Category:** Type of expense (e.g., "Studio Rental", "Equipment", "Marketing")
- **Amount:** How much you spent
- **Description:** What it was for
- **Recurring:** Check if this repeats (e.g., monthly hosting fee)

### 3. **Viewing Your Finances**

**Filter Options:**
- **All:** See both revenue and expenses
- **Revenue:** See only income
- **Expense:** See only spending

**Time Range:**
- **30 Days:** Last month's transactions
- **90 Days:** Last 3 months
- **Year:** Last 12 months

**Summary Cards Show:**
- **Total Revenue:** All money earned
- **Total Expenses:** All money spent
- **Net Profit:** Revenue minus Expenses (your actual profit)

### 4. **Understanding the Dashboard**

**Green Numbers = Good** (Revenue/Profit)
**Red Numbers = Spending** (Expenses)

**Net Profit:**
- **Positive (Green):** You're making more than you're spending ‚úÖ
- **Negative (Red):** You're spending more than you're earning ‚ö†Ô∏è

## Tips for Using the Dashboard

1. **Record Everything:** Add every transaction, no matter how small
2. **Be Consistent:** Update it weekly or after each transaction
3. **Use Clear Categories:** Makes it easier to see where money goes
4. **Review Monthly:** Check your net profit each month
5. **Plan Ahead:** Use recurring expenses to predict future costs
