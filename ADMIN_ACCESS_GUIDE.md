## ğŸ“± Syncing Data Between Devices

### Problem:
When you update settings/videos on your computer, they don't show on your phone (and vice versa).

### Solution: Export/Import Data

#### On Computer (where you made changes):

1. Login to admin panel (triple-tap logo)
2. Go to **Settings** tab
3. Scroll to **"Sync Data Across Devices"** section
4. Click **"Export Data"** button
5. A file will download: `esther-reign-data-[timestamp].json`

#### Transfer the File:

Send the file to your phone using:
- Email (send to yourself)
- WhatsApp (send to yourself)
- Google Drive / Dropbox
- USB cable
- Any file transfer method

#### On Phone (where you want to sync):

1. Open the website on your phone
2. Triple-tap the logo to access admin
3. Login with your password
4. Go to **Settings** tab
5. Scroll to **"Sync Data Across Devices"**
6. Click **"Import Data"** button
7. Select the file you transferred
8. Wait for "Data imported successfully!"
9. Page will reload with all your updates!
